About

In this sample website, I try my best to apply everything that I have learned about HTML, so far.

I learned HTML from Codecademy. I can't claim that I am completely self-taught though. In my journey as I learn about web development, I have a mentor who graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the University of the Philippines (Cebu campus) -- also my alma mater.

My bachelor's degree is in Mass Communication. One may wonder, how is my degree connected to web development? Well, websites are the new medium of communication!

Even the traditional media is online. Newspaper companies now have their own websites from which news articles can be shared to social networking sites.

Summit Media, the Philippine company who published magazines Cosmopolitan, Top Gear, and FHM, has already stopped printing, and has since gone digital.

Radio stations are streaming their shows online while simultaneously being on-air. Some of their shows even uploaded as podcasts.

Content creators from all backgrounds cater to the world's need for diversity. This happens every second of every day.

And all of these communication thrives on beautiful websites. It's a whole new world in here, and I intend to be a part of it.

I don't view my attempt to be a web developer as a shift in career. I am merely expanding my empire, so to speak.

Everything is interconnected. This is what I choose to believe. I connect the dots as I move forward.
